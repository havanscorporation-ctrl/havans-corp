Our Examination Philosophy

At Havans Corporation, examinations are designed around real-life scenarios. Learners are assessed on understanding, reasoning, and application rather than memorisation. Our philosophy promotes competency-based assessment to prepare students for practical challenges beyond the classroom.

Target Learners

These examinations are suitable for Senior 1 to Senior 4 learners, aligned with competency-based curriculum approaches. We aim to support learners in building critical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for academic success and future careers.
